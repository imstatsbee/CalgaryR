Be Our Speaker

CalgaryR is looking for a voluntary speaker who would like to provide a tutorial, introduce an interesting problem, or build a partnership to work together. The examples are as below:

  • Applications of statistical modelling, data mining, and machine learning techniques
  • Anything that R cannot do or R is not efficient on your work
  • Techniques needed for big data analysis, management, and data cleaning, etc
  • Software engineerning needed for integrating R in other environment, objective-oriented programming, package development and documentation, debugging, and interfacing with other languages, etc.
  • Advanced techniques in reproducible research, parallel computing, scientific computing, and operational research, etc.
  • Viusalization method and communication
